🎗 AJCC Cancer Staging for "Breast Cancer" 🎗

ENTER

RESULT

Tumor (T)

✏ T1: Tumor ≤20 mm in greatest dimension.

✏ T2: Tumor >20 mm but ≤50 mm in greatest dimension.

✏ T3: Tumor >50 mm in greatest dimension.

✏ T4: Tumor of any size with direct extension to chest wall and/or skin.

✏ T4A: Extension to the chest wall (excluding pectoralis muscle).

✏ T4B: Ulceration, macroscopic satellite nodules, or edema.

✏ T4C: Both T4A and T4B features present.

Node (N)

✏ N0: No regional lymph node metastases.

✏ N1: Movable ipsilateral Level I or II axillary lymph node(s).

✏ N2: Fixed/matted axillary nodes or internal mammary nodes.

✏ N3: Involvement of infraclavicular, supraclavicular, or combined nodes.

Metastasis (M)

✏ M0: No distant metastases.

✏ M1: Distant metastases present.
